Who We Are

About WMAA

WMAA is an ACNC-registered charity dedicated to supporting culturally and linguistically diverse (CALD) communities across Western Australia. We provide practical help, cultural understanding, and support for migrants, students, visitors, and families in need.

We work collaboratively with universities, community organisations, service providers, and government agencies to build a strong support network for CALD individuals.

How We Work

Our Approach

Our services are both proactive and responsive.

Providing immediate crisis assistance, housing referrals, and advocacy.

Delivering education and awareness programs that prevent harm and build resilience.

Acting as cultural mediators to bridge communication gaps between families, services, and authorities.

The Future

Looking Ahead

WMAA will continue to expand its services by building on these four pillars.

01
Strengthening Partnerships

Collaborating with local and international organisations to expand our reach and improve support for CALD communities.

02
Raising Community Awareness

Promoting greater understanding of available services so individuals know where to turn for help.

03
Building a Skilled Volunteer Network

Training volunteers in cultural mediation, emergency response, and advocacy to deliver compassionate assistance.

04
Developing Tailored Support Programs

Creating specialised initiatives for vulnerable groups, including victims of family violence, fraud, and housing insecurity.
