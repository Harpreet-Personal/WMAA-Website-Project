Service 01

Crisis Response & Emergency Support

Providing immediate assistance to individuals and families in crisis, ensuring access to essential resources and urgent care when it's needed most.

Our crisis team responds rapidly to situations involving housing instability, family breakdown, medical emergencies, and other urgent welfare needs within CALD communities.

Get Support
24/7 emergency referral support
Coordination with emergency services
Access to food, clothing & essentials
Family crisis counselling referrals
Safe house and shelter connections

Service 02

Cultural Mediation & Family Liaison

Bridging cultural and language gaps to help families communicate effectively with service providers, schools, and community organisations.

Our trained cultural mediators work directly with families, schools, courts, and welfare agencies to ensure every individual is heard and understood.

Request a Mediator
Multilingual mediators available
School and education liaison
Court and legal support facilitation
Government agency communication
Family conflict resolution support

Service 03

Temporary Accommodation Referrals

Connecting those experiencing hardship with safe and suitable short-term housing options through our trusted network of partners.

We work with shelters, faith communities, and housing providers to ensure no one in our community is left without a safe place to stay.

Find Housing Help
Emergency accommodation referrals
Transitional housing connections
Culturally appropriate placements
Support for families with children
Ongoing follow-up and welfare checks

Service 04

Anti-Fraud & Safety Awareness Programs

Educating community members on protecting themselves from scams, exploitation, and unsafe situations through targeted awareness initiatives.

CALD communities are often targeted by sophisticated scams. Our programs build awareness and resilience through workshops, resources, and direct outreach.

Learn More
Community fraud awareness workshops
Online safety and scam education
Financial exploitation prevention
Multilingual resources and materials
Referral to police and support services

Service 05

Health & Welfare Advocacy

Supporting individuals in navigating healthcare and social services, ensuring fair access to essential wellbeing and welfare resources.

We advocate for CALD community members within the healthcare system, helping them understand their rights and access the services they are entitled to.

Get Advocacy Support
Hospital and GP navigation support
Mental health service referrals
Centrelink and welfare guidance
NDIS access support
Health interpreter coordination

Service 06

Sustainability

Ensuring lasting benefits for communities through programs designed with long-term impact and resilience in mind.

Our sustainability initiatives focus on empowering communities to support themselves, reducing dependency and building long-term capacity within CALD groups.

Get Involved
Community capacity building programs
Volunteer recruitment and training
Peer support network development
Partnership and funding programs
Leadership and skills development
